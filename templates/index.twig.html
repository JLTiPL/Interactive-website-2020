We are Interactive

JLT Interactive (Interactive) is the digital solution provider specializing in Insurance and Risk Management software solutions and products development. Interactive focuses exclusively on technology enablement of our services for our large corporate clients worldwide. Large enterprises with geographically diverse portfolio with a risk management focus and data handling needs leverage on our flagship solution to improve the efficiency of its insurance operations and to enhance their management of enterprise risk.

  • Consolidate responsibilities
  • Obtain real-time access to enterprise-wide information for enhanced planning and effective decisions
  • Improve collaboration among internal and external stakeholders

Interactive was established as an independent company on April 2005. Our products have mostly evolved over the years based on demands from various clients.

We build easy-to-use customised software solutions that take the hassle out of business operations – so you can focus on the things that matter.

The interactive technology solutions help our clients consolidate responsibilities in the context of insurance buying and Enterprise Risk Management. It further assists in improving the collaboration between internal and external stakeholders. The systems provide real time access to group wide information for better planning and decision making.
