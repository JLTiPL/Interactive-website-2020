About iCargo

iCargo is our proprietary, Online cargo insurance management system to manage end to end Cargo Insurance including client's management. iCargo is an integrated comprehensive end-to-end solution. It provides a clear, easy-to-use, web-based administrative structure to streamline business process, increase productivity, improve record keeping and enhance data analytics.

Our eight modules can be configured to your specific needs - and mixed and matched to individual specifications. iCargo is designed to add comvenience to processes such as:

Claims
Reporting
Transactions
Document and Useful Links Depository
Incident tracking
Dashboard Oversight
On-line accounting
Warehouse Self-assessment

What can iCargo do?

Built specifically for the Marine Cargo business, iCargo is a multi-language cradle-to-grave system that can handle all aspects of the claims process system, including but not limited to: producing Certificates of Insurance, recording individual shipment data, shipment and warehousing data; bulk uploading transactions; and pricing premium and calculating transactions.

The system automates many of these processes and keeps all relevant parties are notified at each stage. You can also view information in real-time for increased transparency and productivity.

iCargo is an ecosystem built around a community of active international and local insurance players that includes loss adjustors, recovery agents and brokers with whom JLT has held a long-established partnership.
