About Insure Portal

Insure portal is an online insurance full turnkey portal, provides start up’s and SME’s in the UAE with access to comprehensive, competitively priced insurance solutions online, from the convenience of their home or office. The portal allows the user to purchase Health and Business insurance policies with in UAE for Onshore OR Free zone Companies. Easy-to-use portal enables you to access business insurance comparison quotes, pay online, and download your policy all within 5 minutes. For health insurance; policies can be tailored to meet client business requirements and budget and manage member additions, cancellations and policy renewal from a single platform – simple, quick and convenient.

The solution is integrated with dynamic rate engine generation and real time API functionality to get quote from the Insurer Partners, perform payment transaction (Payment Gateway Service Provider). The system is facilitated to store documents in Document Management System (integrated with SharePoint).
