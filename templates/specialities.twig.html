Quality
Be assured of reliable and secure applications built on a foundation of rigorous quality testing
Interactive takes quality to heart. All solutions and software applications undergo end-to-end functional and performance testing. This is done by our Software Testing and Assure Reliability (STAR) Team.
"We assure quality and reliability. We are always dedicated to deliver stable and secure software applications with strict adherence to agreed timelines."
We cover System Testing, System Integration Testing, A/B Testing, Non-Functional Testing, Performance Testing and Security Testing. User Acceptance Testing (UAT) is crucial for the successful implementation. Our testing experts and business analysts, with their strong domain and business knowledge, work closely with and assist our business and client users in UAT. We take a pragmatic view on Testing Automation and focusing on the automation only where a strong case and clear ROI.
As part of our organization ongoing service quality assurance, a mandatory vulnerability testing is conducted on across all of our testing composites.
