Quality

Interactive takes quality to heart. All solutions and software applications undergo end-to-end functional and performance testing. This is done by our Software Testing and Assure Reliability (STAR) Team.

“We assure quality and reliability and always are dedicated to deliver stable and secure software applications with strict adherence to agreed timelines.”

Our testing includes:

  • Test schedules dates
  • SIT and UAT test cases
  • System testing
  • System integration testing
  • Data and technology migration testing
  • Non-functional testing / A/B testing
  • Performance and Security testing

As part of our organization ongoing service quality assurance, a mandatory vulnerability testing is conducted on across all of our testing composites.

Knowledge Management

Interactive proprietary secured online portal for risk and insurance information. The portal consolidate and enable readily access to all relevant insurance and claims documents and make it easy to perform key functions effectively and efficiently anywhere and anytime.

“Fingertip access to quick domain related knowledge and updates on the go”

The fundamental design principle of Knowledge Management is to deliver value to the client in a rapid and scalable manner where integration with our in-house eco-system of technological solutions offering can be enabled.

Knowledge Management serves as a platform to upload and share industry relevant documents with client users. This means brokers and clients can access their insurance details in real-time; be it a review of current premiums or downloading a policy details.

The portal implements a role based security model where user(s) will have access only to information they are privileged to have. The system also implements standard industry strength security protocols for data at rest and in transit.
