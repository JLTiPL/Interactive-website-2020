About Benpal Asia

Benpal Asia is an innovative platform, which helps organizations to gain the same level of control over their benefits schemes and data as they have in their finance and human resources areas. It enables companies of all sizes to manage their benefit schemes more efficiently, driving better value from the spend whilst enhancing communication and understanding. It is a single integrated platform for the complete management and administration of employee benefits. It uses an online shopping cart concept that is easy-to-use and familiar to most users. The interface is simple to navigate and provides clear benefit descriptions and selection options. Benpal Asia is the next generation rewards, compensations and benefits platform, designed to help sustain, attract, retain, engage and reward your valuable employees and manage your employee rewards and benefit programs.

Employee Benefits are one of the important parts of any compensation package. Benefits help employees feel secured in multiple aspects of their career life by helping them manage the cost of healthcare and enjoy work life balance. When your employees feel secure, they perform better and stays longer in a company. This creates a positive work environment, helping companies better achieve their goals. Diverse organizations require different types of benefits and requirements to provide strategic compensations to their employees. A simple yet versatile system is beneficial to companies nowadays to provide the best strategic benefits for their employees.

Key features of an Employee Benefit platform:

Benpal is an all in one platform that not only allows Employees to view their allocated Core Benefits but also provides an option to enroll voluntary products as well as upgrade existing benefits. With the Flexible Benefits feature, Employees can efficiently utilize their allocated points by submitting reimbursements through the system. Employee can add dependents and they can also be easily managed in the system as Benpal supports not just the employees but the benefits for dependents too.
