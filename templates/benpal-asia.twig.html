About Benpal Asia

Benpal Asia is online benefits management system that has been designed to offer a user-friendly way to understand their employment benefits as well as select and manage their benefit options. The portal's flexible functionalities allow it to be configured for different Asia employment benefit markets and serve these comprehensively. What can Benpal Asia do?

Benpal Asia manages and updates all employee and dependant data online, allowing a more efficient, automated way of keeping records up to date. Data changes can also be tracked through an extensive audit log facility. As a single congruent platform, the system also seamlessly integrates all the different benefits functions into one portal and automatically allocates the appropriate benefit plan to each member. The portal can also be configured for different Asian employment benefit markets and facilitate the hassle-free roll out country-specific benefits. Many other functions that used to require manual checks or processes can also be automated. These include the ability to generate employee/dependant listings, medical cards and weekly reports. Underwriting requirements can also be automatically validated. A key time- and resource-saving feature is the self-service feature, where employees can enrol themselves for voluntary covers, search for their specific benefits, access forms and resources and select their own benefits. This reduces the volume of employee queries and administrative support.
